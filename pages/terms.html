Terms & Privacy

What information we collect, why we collect it, and how you stay in control of it.

Last updated: July 2026

Who We Are

ResearchBridge Consulting ("we", "us", "our") provides research support, data analysis training, and academic editing services, based in Kigali, Rwanda. This page explains what happens to your information when you create an account, enrol in a course, or request one of our services.

Information We Collect

Depending on which forms you use, we collect:

  • Account details — full name, email address, phone/WhatsApp number, and country, when you sign up or enrol.
  • Enrolment details — the course, mode (online or in-person), category, and experience level you select.
  • Documents you submit — if you use our Thesis Editing & Proofreading service, the files you upload for review.
  • Learning activity — your progress through course lessons and quizzes, so we can track completion and issue certificates.
  • Communications — messages you send us through the contact form, WhatsApp, or email.

We never ask for payment card details through this website — payment is confirmed directly with our team after your quote or enrolment is reviewed.

How We Use It

  • To create and manage your account, and to confirm enrolments and service requests.
  • To deliver course content, track your progress, and issue completion certificates.
  • To communicate with you about your enrolment, request, or account — including confirmation emails and, where a course isn't finished, occasional progress reminders.
  • To send you ResearchBridge updates, news, and announcements — only if you've opted in to marketing communications.

Third Parties That Process Your Data

We keep the number of third parties small, and only use services required to run the platform:

  • Supabase — hosts our database and handles account authentication (sign up, log in, password reset).
  • Resend — delivers transactional emails (welcome messages, enrolment confirmations, progress reminders).
  • Netlify and Render — host the website and backend server respectively.

We do not sell your information to anyone.

Marketing Communications

If you check the "Keep me updated" box on our signup or enrolment forms, we'll occasionally send you news about new courses, services, and announcements. This is entirely optional and separate from the account or service emails you'll receive regardless (like enrolment confirmations) — you can opt out of marketing messages at any time by contacting us.

Data Retention & Security

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service you've requested. Access to our systems is restricted to authorised ResearchBridge staff.

Your Rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time by emailing info@researchbridgeconsulting.com. You can also update most of your details yourself from your account's Profile page.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to how we handle your information, we'll update this page and, where appropriate, let you know directly.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Reach us at info@researchbridgeconsulting.com or via WhatsApp.
