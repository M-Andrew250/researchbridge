Capacity Building & Training

Tailored research and data skills training for teams, organisations, and institutions — delivered online or in-person in Kigali and across the region.

Overview

What Is This Service?

Many organisations collect data but lack the internal skills to analyse it effectively, or have staff who need to strengthen their research capabilities to perform better in their roles. Our Capacity Building and Training service fills that gap.

We design and deliver customised training programmes for NGOs, government institutions, and corporate teams — covering everything from research fundamentals to advanced data analytics tools. All training is practical, hands-on, and tailored to your organisation's specific context and goals.

Deliverables

What's Included

🎯

Needs Assessment

We assess your team's current skills and gaps to design a training programme that delivers maximum impact.

📐

Custom Training Design

Training content tailored to your sector, data context, and the specific tools your team uses or needs.

💻

Hands-on Tool Training

Practical sessions on Excel, SPSS, Stata, R, Python, Power BI, KoBoToolbox, NVivo, and more.

📊

Research Methods Training

Training on research design, data collection, analysis, and reporting for non-specialist staff and teams.

📁

Training Materials

All participants receive printed or digital training manuals, exercises, and reference materials to keep.

🏅

Certificate of Completion

Participants receive a ResearchBridge certificate upon successful completion of the training programme.

Target Clients

Who Is This For?

Any organisation that wants to invest in the research and data skills of its people.

  • NGOs and development organisations
  • Government ministries and public institutions
  • Corporate teams and private sector organisations
  • Universities and academic departments
  • Community based organisations and cooperatives

Process

How It Works

1
Training Needs Assessment

We meet with you to understand your team's skills, gaps, and training goals.

2
Programme Design

We design a customised training plan with sessions, tools, and outcomes.

3
Training Delivery

We deliver the training online or in-person with hands-on exercises.

4
Evaluation & Certificates

We evaluate learning outcomes and issue certificates to participants.
