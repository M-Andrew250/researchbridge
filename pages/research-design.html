Research Project Design

Build a solid foundation for your research — from defining your objectives and choosing the right methodology to designing your sampling strategy and data collection plan.

Overview

What Is This Service?

A well-designed research project is the difference between findings that are credible and findings that fall apart under scrutiny. Our Research Project Design service helps students, researchers, NGOs, and institutions build rigorous, well-structured research frameworks from the ground up.

We work with you to clarify your research problem, define your objectives, select the most appropriate methodology, and design every component of your study before data collection begins — saving you time, cost, and frustration down the line.

Deliverables

What's Included

🎯

Problem & Objectives

Clarification of your research problem, research questions, and measurable objectives aligned to your study goals.

🗺️

Conceptual Framework

Development of a conceptual or theoretical framework that grounds your study in existing knowledge.

⚙️

Methodology Selection

Guidance on choosing between qualitative, quantitative, or mixed methods approaches for your specific context.

👥

Sampling Strategy

Design of your sampling approach — population definition, sample size calculation, and sampling technique selection.

📋

Data Collection Plan

A structured plan detailing what data you need, how you will collect it, and what tools you will use.

📄

Research Proposal

A complete written research proposal ready for submission to your institution, supervisor, or funding body.

Target Clients

Who Is This For?

Anyone starting a research project who wants to get the design right from the beginning.

  • Students developing thesis or dissertation proposals
  • NGOs designing programme evaluations
  • Government institutions planning policy research
  • Researchers applying for grants or funding
  • Organisations conducting internal research studies

Process

How It Works

1
Initial Consultation

We discuss your research topic, context, and goals to understand what you need.

2
Framework Development

We develop your conceptual framework, objectives, and methodology together.

3
Design & Documentation

We document the full research design into a structured proposal or plan.

4
Review & Finalise

We review the design with you and refine until you are fully confident.
