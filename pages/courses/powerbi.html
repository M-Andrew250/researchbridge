Overview

About This Course

Microsoft Power BI is the world's leading business intelligence and data visualisation platform. Used by millions of professionals across every industry, Power BI allows you to connect to any data source, transform and model your data, and build stunning interactive dashboards that can be shared across your organisation.

This course takes you from connecting your first dataset through to building advanced DAX measures and publishing professional dashboards — with every session grounded in real business and research scenarios.

Course Outline

Module Breakdown

Module 1 Power BI Foundations +
  • Installing Power BI Desktop and overview of the interface
  • Connecting to Excel, CSV, and web data sources
  • Loading and refreshing data in Power BI
  • Understanding the Report, Data, and Model views
  • Building your first simple report
Module 2 Data Transformation with Power Query +
  • Introduction to Power Query Editor
  • Removing errors, blanks, and duplicates
  • Splitting, merging, and pivoting columns
  • Appending and merging queries
  • Creating custom columns and conditional logic
Module 3 Data Modelling & Relationships +
  • Understanding star and snowflake schemas
  • Creating relationships between tables
  • Introduction to DAX — calculated columns vs measures
  • Key DAX functions — SUM, CALCULATE, FILTER, DIVIDE
  • Time intelligence — YTD, MTD, and period comparisons
Module 4 Dashboard Design & Visualisations +
  • Choosing the right chart for your data
  • Bar, line, pie, scatter, and map visuals
  • KPI cards, gauges, and tables
  • Slicers, filters, and cross-filtering between visuals
  • Dashboard design principles and formatting
Module 5 Publishing & Sharing +
  • Publishing reports to Power BI Service
  • Creating and sharing dashboards online
  • Row-level security for data access control
  • Embedding reports in websites and Microsoft Teams
  • Scheduling automatic data refresh

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Power BI free to use? +

Power BI Desktop is completely free to download and use. Power BI Service has a free tier with limited sharing features. We cover both during the course and guide you on licensing options.

Do I need Excel knowledge before joining? +

Basic Excel familiarity is helpful since Power BI often connects to Excel files. However the course starts from basics and no advanced Excel knowledge is required.

Will I get a certificate? +

Yes. All participants who complete the course and practical assessment receive an official ResearchBridge Certificate of Completion.

Can my organisation book this for a team? +

Yes. We offer group pricing for teams of 5 or more. Contact us for a custom quote and scheduling that suits your organisation.
