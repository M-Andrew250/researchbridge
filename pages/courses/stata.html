Overview

About This Course

Stata is the gold standard tool for econometric analysis and quantitative social science research. Used by economists, policy analysts, and researchers at universities, central banks, and international organisations worldwide, Stata combines powerful statistical capabilities with a clean, reproducible workflow.

This course takes you from Stata basics through to advanced regression and panel data modelling — with every session grounded in real research datasets and practical do-file writing.

Course Outline

Module Breakdown

Module 1 Stata Foundations +
  • Introduction to the Stata interface
  • Do-files, log files, and reproducible workflows
  • Importing data from Excel, CSV, and other formats
  • Browsing, listing, and summarising data
  • Variable types and basic data operations
Module 2 Data Management +
  • Generating and recoding variables
  • Labelling variables and values
  • Merging and appending datasets
  • Reshaping data — wide to long and back
  • Handling missing data and outliers
Module 3 Descriptive Statistics & Graphics +
  • Summarise, tabulate, and cross-tabulate
  • Means, frequencies, and distributions
  • T-tests, chi-square, and ANOVA
  • Histograms, scatter plots, and box plots
  • Exporting publication-quality graphs
Module 4 Regression Analysis +
  • OLS regression — running and interpreting
  • Robust standard errors and heteroskedasticity
  • Logit and probit models for binary outcomes
  • Post-estimation commands and marginal effects
  • Exporting regression tables with outreg2
Module 5 Panel Data & Advanced Models +
  • Setting up panel data with xtset
  • Fixed effects and random effects models
  • Hausman test for model selection
  • Introduction to instrumental variables
  • Difference-in-differences estimation

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need Stata installed before the course starts? +

Yes. Stata is paid software but offers student licences at reduced cost. We will guide you on obtaining and installing it before the first session. Free trial versions are also available.

Do I need statistics knowledge before joining? +

Basic statistics knowledge is helpful but not required. We cover the statistical concepts needed alongside the Stata commands so you understand both the tool and the method.

Will I get a certificate? +

Yes. All participants who complete the course and practical assessment receive an official ResearchBridge Certificate of Completion.

Can my organisation book this for a team? +

Yes. We offer group pricing for teams of 5 or more. Contact us for a custom quote and scheduling that suits your organisation.
