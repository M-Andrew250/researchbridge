Overview

About This Course

KoBoToolbox is the world's most widely used free platform for humanitarian data collection and field surveys. Trusted by the UN, Red Cross, MSF, World Bank, and thousands of NGOs and research organisations globally, KoBoToolbox allows you to design powerful digital surveys, deploy them online or offline, and collect data directly from mobile devices in the field.

This course takes you from creating your first form through to advanced skip logic, repeat groups, media questions, and data export — giving you everything you need to run professional field data collection operations.

Course Outline

Module Breakdown

Module 1 KoBoToolbox Foundations +
  • Introduction to digital data collection and KoBoToolbox
  • Setting up your account and navigating the platform
  • Understanding the form builder interface
  • Question types — text, number, select one, select many
  • Previewing and testing your first form
Module 2 Advanced Form Design +
  • Skip logic and relevance conditions
  • Validation rules and response constraints
  • Repeat groups for household roster data
  • GPS, photo, audio, and video questions
  • Cascading select — linked dropdown questions
Module 3 Deployment & Field Data Collection +
  • Deploying surveys via web link and QR code
  • Setting up the KoBoCollect app on Android devices
  • Offline data collection without internet access
  • Managing enumerators and team permissions
  • Monitoring incoming submissions in real time
Module 4 Data Management & Export +
  • Reviewing and validating submitted data
  • Editing and correcting submissions
  • Exporting data to Excel, CSV, SPSS, and other formats
  • Using the built-in reports and summary charts
  • Connecting KoBoToolbox data to Power BI and other tools

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is KoBoToolbox free to use? +

Yes. KoBoToolbox is completely free for NGOs, researchers, and humanitarian organisations. A paid plan is available for private sector users needing higher submission volumes.

Do I need technical skills to join? +

No technical skills are required. KoBoToolbox has a user-friendly interface and the course starts from absolute basics. If you can use a smartphone you can learn KoBoToolbox.

Can I collect data without internet access? +

Yes. KoBoCollect on Android devices allows fully offline data collection. Data syncs automatically to the server when connectivity is restored. We cover this in Module 3.

Will I get a certificate? +

Yes. All participants who complete the course and practical assessment receive an official ResearchBridge Certificate of Completion.

Can my organisation book this for a field team? +

Absolutely. We regularly train full field teams for NGOs and research organisations. Contact us for group pricing and we can deliver the training at your offices.
