Overview

About This Course

This course is designed for anyone who works with data and wants to use Excel confidently and efficiently. Whether you are a student organising research data, a professional building reports, or an NGO staff member tracking programme data — this course will take you from basic to advanced Excel skills in four practical weeks.

Every session is hands-on. You learn by doing — working with real datasets and building tools you can use immediately in your work.

Course Outline

Module Breakdown

Module 1 Excel Foundations +
  • Introduction to the Excel interface and ribbon
  • Creating, saving, and managing workbooks
  • Entering and formatting data in cells
  • Working with rows, columns, and sheets
  • Basic arithmetic and cell referencing
Module 2 Formulas & Functions +
  • SUM, AVERAGE, COUNT, MIN, MAX
  • IF, AND, OR logical functions
  • VLOOKUP and HLOOKUP
  • INDEX and MATCH
  • Text functions: CONCATENATE, LEFT, RIGHT, MID
  • Date and time functions
Module 3 Data Cleaning & Management +
  • Sorting and filtering data
  • Removing duplicates and handling missing values
  • Data validation and error checking
  • Find, replace, and text to columns
  • Freezing panes and managing large datasets
Module 4 Pivot Tables & Charts +
  • Creating and configuring pivot tables
  • Grouping, filtering, and summarising data
  • Building pivot charts from pivot tables
  • Creating bar, line, pie, and scatter charts
  • Formatting charts for professional presentation
Module 5 Dashboards & Reporting +
  • Introduction to dashboard design principles
  • Using slicers and timelines for interactivity
  • Combining charts and tables into a dashboard
  • Conditional formatting for data highlights
  • Preparing and printing professional reports

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior Excel knowledge to join? +

No prior knowledge is required. The course starts from the very basics and builds progressively. Complete beginners are very welcome.

What do I need to participate online? +

You need a laptop or desktop computer with Microsoft Excel installed, a stable internet connection, and a Zoom or Google Meet account for live sessions.

Will I get a certificate after completing the course? +

Yes. All participants who complete the course and the practical assessment receive a ResearchBridge Consulting Certificate of Completion.

Can my organisation book the course for a team? +

Absolutely. We offer group pricing for teams of 5 or more. Contact us for a custom quote and we can schedule sessions at a time that suits your team.

How long do I have access to the course materials? +

All participants receive digital training materials that are theirs to keep permanently after the course.
