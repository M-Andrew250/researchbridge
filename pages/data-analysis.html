Data Collection & Analysis

End-to-end research data support — from designing your data collection instruments to delivering clean, analysed results ready for reporting.

Overview

What Is This Service?

Our Data Collection and Analysis service supports researchers, students, NGOs, and institutions at every stage of the data pipeline. Whether you need help designing a survey, collecting field data, cleaning messy datasets, or running statistical analyses, we provide expert hands-on support using industry standard tools.

We work with both quantitative and qualitative data across a wide range of research contexts — from academic studies to organisational evaluations.

Deliverables

What's Included

📋

Survey & Instrument Design

Design of questionnaires, interview guides, and data collection tools tailored to your research objectives.

🌐

Digital Data Collection

Setup and deployment of digital data collection using KoBoToolbox, Google Forms, or ODK for field surveys.

🧹

Data Cleaning

Identification and correction of errors, duplicates, missing values, and inconsistencies in your dataset.

📈

Statistical Analysis

Descriptive and inferential analysis using SPSS, Stata, R, Python, or Excel depending on your needs.

📊

Data Visualisation

Charts, graphs, and dashboards that communicate your findings clearly to any audience.

📄

Results Report

A structured report presenting your findings with tables, visuals, and plain language interpretation.

Target Clients

Who Is This For?

Anyone who works with data and needs expert support at any stage of the process.

  • Students conducting primary research
  • NGOs running field surveys or evaluations
  • Government institutions collecting public data
  • Businesses analysing operational or market data
  • Researchers needing statistical analysis support
